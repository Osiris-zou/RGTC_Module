Note on manuscript rounding: the manuscript reports aAcc, mAcc, and mIoU to three decimal places. The `Delta mIoU vs. ToMe` values shown in the manuscript are computed from these displayed three-decimal mIoU values, whereas this CSV retains full-precision raw metrics. Recomputing the differences from the full-precision CSV values gives small last-decimal changes, such as -0.029 instead of -0.028 at r=16 and +0.025 instead of +0.026 at r=48. These differences are caused only by rounding convention and do not affect the reported conclusion.
